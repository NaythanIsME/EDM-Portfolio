Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

by Sing Shong

Chapter 12 – “A Shared Secret”

The train was silent, but not because of peace. The air was tense with uncertainty, as if even the oxygen had paused to read the next line.

Kim Dokja “You saw it too, didn’t you? The message window. It’s not just me anymore.”
Han Sooyoung “I always figured the world would go to hell someday. I just didn’t think it’d be this well-written.”

Her voice was light, but her eyes scanned the darkened tunnel with caution. Even she couldn’t joke her way through this for long.

Kim Dokja “You think this is fiction. But we’re in the story now. We don’t get to be readers anymore.”
Han Sooyoung “Says the guy who’s quoting his favorite novel while the world collapses. What, are you the main character now?”

Kim Dokja didn’t answer. His eyes were locked on the glowing blue message hovering mid-air.
