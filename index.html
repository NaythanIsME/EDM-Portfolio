[#001: Prologue - A Single Reader's Perspective]

As always, the subway was packed. The fluorescent lights above flickered slightly, casting pale shadows across the exhausted faces of the evening crowd.

Kim Dokja glanced down at his phone, the screen glowing softly with the text of Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse. No one else in this train car knew that the world would end in exactly seven minutes.

“You’re still reading that weird webnovel?”
— Yoo Sangah

He smiled faintly. The words on his screen shifted as the latest chapter updated in real time—a chapter that should not exist.

[The scenario is beginning.]

The lights went out.

When they came back, the train had stopped, and something was standing on the tracks. Something not human.

“This isn’t fiction anymore.”
— Kim Dokja
